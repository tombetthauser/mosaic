Betthauser Mosaics
  Jon Betthauser

Jon Betthauser is the chief creative director, director of design and founder of Betthauser Mosaics. Over the past thirty years he has travelled the world as an exhibit designer and design consultant for some of the most influential science museums in the country including the California Academy of Sciences and the Tech Museum of Innovation.

In 2007 Jon began mosaic work for clients and has since developed a passion an expertise in the technical processes and aesthetic nuances necessary for the design, fabrication and installation of large and medium scale mosaic projects for public and private venues.   Tom Betthauser

Tom Betthauser acts as Betthauser Mosaic's chief creative and technical assistant. He has worked in a wide range of positions and departments at science museums since 2006 including the Exploratorium and The Natural History Museum of Los Angeles.

Since recieving his master's degree from Yale University's School of Art in 2012 he has devoted his efforts to teaching Art History and Studio Practices while independently developing a body of web-based interactive projects that make full use of a contemporary web development stack including ES6 and ReactJS.
